Michael Macaulay began his career in his bedroom at 12 years old. He taught himself video editing to become a gaming superstar.


He launched a horror narration channel in early 2015. Operating under the alias Erick Alden, he created hundreds of videos. After gaining over 80K subscribers, he sold the channel in 2018 to pursue his true passion - History.


History for Thinkers was born in early 2020. This show doesn’t just teach history. It covers philosophy, economics, personal liberation, and much more. And it’s all done through history.
