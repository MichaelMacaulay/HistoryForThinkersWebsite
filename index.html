History. A word loaded with ideas. We forget what it really means sometimes. Here’s the dictionary definition, “the study of past events, particularly in human affairs.” But I’d like to propose a new definition. A different way to study history - “to learn lessons from the past and use them.” Emphasis on using these lessons. There needs to be a purpose behind studying the past. If not, we’re just wasting our own time. And unfortunately, this is what many decent people do. But there is a better way.
