Created by Michael Macaulay

Michael started History for Thinkers in 2018. The early episodes were biography-heavy and included names like Napoleon, Hitler and Stalin. To spice things up, he covered writers too. Lovecraft and Poe. The show has covered Mein Kampf, the communist manifesto, and much more.
In 2021 everything changed. The show branched out and started covering historical events, psychology and more. All episodes are available on YouTube with visuals.
